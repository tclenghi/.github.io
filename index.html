About me

I obtained a Ph.D. in Economics from Purdue University in August 2022, an M.S. in Statistics and a B.S. in Finance from University of Science and Technology of China in June 2016 and June 2014, respectively. My academic field is Econometrics. I am also interested in Finance and Macroeconomics, where flexible and powerful time series/panel data tools can be applied to answer empirical research questions.

Research

My research interests include:

  • Nonstationary Time Series: Structural Breaks, Robust Inference, etc.

  • Large Vector Autoregressions (VAR).

  • Dynamic Panel Data Models​.

  • Model Averaging.
